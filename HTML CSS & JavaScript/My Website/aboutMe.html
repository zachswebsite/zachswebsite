About Me
Hi, I'm Zach and I write code.
I was born and raised in good-ole Kansas. I moved to Colorado for highschool, and then California (UCSC) for my first year of College. Currently, I am back in Colorado as a student at Denver University, majoring in Video Game Design BS.
Growing up in Kansas, a large part of our entertainment was corn field mazes and video games. When I ran out of mazes, I discovered the beautiful artwork that is Video Games. I loved music, books, and movies at the time. Unlike many art forms, video games can be a culmination of multiple arts at their best. The most memorable video games generally have captivating storylines, catching music, and user interactivity to challenge you. Video Games create the best entertainment escapism for kids and adults that we have to date. As someone who treasures all the memories and escapism video games have brought me, I want to give back to the world of video games with my own ideas.
